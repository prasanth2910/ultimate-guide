Description

Rajasthan is a state in northern India known for its storied past, colorful culture, and architectural marvels. The state is home to several historic cities, including Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Udaipur, known for their palaces, forts, and temples. Rajasthan is also famous for its vibrant festivals, traditional arts, and delicious cuisine.

Famous For

  • Historic cities and forts
  • Colorful culture and traditions
  • Traditional arts and crafts
