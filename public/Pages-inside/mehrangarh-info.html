  1. Home
  2. Mehrangarh Fort

Description

Mehrangarh Fort is one of the largest forts in India, located in Jodhpur, Rajasthan. It is situated 410 feet (125 meters) above the city and is enclosed by imposing thick walls. The fort houses several palaces known for their intricate carvings and expansive courtyards. Mehrangarh Fort offers panoramic views of Jodhpur's blue houses and the surrounding landscape.

Famous For

  • Impressive architecture and design
  • Historical significance
  • Scenic views of Jodhpur
