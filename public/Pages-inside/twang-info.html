Description

Twang is a town in the northeastern part of the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh. It is located at an elevation of 3,048 meters (10,000 feet) and is known for its scenic beauty and Buddhist monasteries. Twang is also famous for being the birthplace of the 6th Dalai Lama.

Famous For

  • Buddhist monasteries
  • Birthplace of the 6th Dalai Lama
  • Scenic beauty
