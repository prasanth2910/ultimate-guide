Description

The Vivekananda Rock Memorial is a popular tourist monument in Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu, India. It stands on a rock island, about 500 meters off the mainland, and is dedicated to the Indian saint Swami Vivekananda. The memorial consists of two main structures, the Vivekananda Mandapam and the Shripada Mandapam, both of which are architectural marvels. The memorial is a symbol of unity and brotherhood and is visited by thousands of tourists and pilgrims every year.

Famous For
